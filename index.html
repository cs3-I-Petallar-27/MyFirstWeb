MY POEM

Whispers of the Forest

by: Ishe Braelyn E. Petallar

In the heart of the rich green wood,

The leaves, they whisper, stories uncounted,

In type's mysteries, my inspiration unfolds.

Majestic trees, their weaponry outspread,

A shelter for dreams in their shaded bed,

With all step, a fiction unfurls,

In the whispers of the wood, my soul twirls.
